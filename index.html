What does GreeniPhi do?

GreeniPhi delivers logistics, marketing and resale support tailored to your campus or Greek organization to make our recycling for reuse program a rewarding and valuable experience for you.

We work

  1. to divert students’ and alumni’s reusable items from the landfill
  2. to prevent additional resource consumption by selling collected items to students and community members in order
  3. to raise funds for GreeniPhi Preferred Charities and Organizations.

Our Partnerships: GreeniPhi Preferred Charities and Organizations

GreeniPhi preferred charities and organizations are a distinct group of Greek, charitable and educational organizations that have joined forces with GreeniPhi to process apparel, school supplies and home goods donations on behalf of their donors and members.

GreeniPhi delivers sustainability reports demonstrating the social and economic benefits of sustainability at every step of the way.

Why GreeniPhi?

GreeniPhi has created an innovative recycling and fundraising solution that offers college students, Greek organizations and universities a sustainable solution for cleaning out dorms and apartments that increase college affordability.
